Precision Thermal Analysis

The Home Energy Forecast Report (HEFR) blends experimental building science with rigorous thermal-fluid modeling to optimize building envelopes, window performance, and HVAC efficiency.

By leveraging advanced physics-based simulations alongside high-accuracy environmental data, we provide actionable, engineering-grade forecasting to minimize thermal losses and maximize long-term system durability.

What the Report Covers

Whether analyzing fenestration performance under localized pressure and altitude changes or calculating complex whole-home heat transfer coefficients, our technical approach removes the guesswork from residential efficiency improvements.
